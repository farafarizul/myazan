**myAzan Privacy Policy**

Effective date: [insert date]

myAzan is a Windows desktop application that helps users play the azan automatically based on JAKIM prayer times in Malaysia. We respect user privacy and are committed to clearly explaining how the app handles data.

### 1. Information handled by the app

myAzan is designed to operate primarily on the user’s local device. The app may store the following information locally on the user’s computer:

* app settings such as the selected prayer time zone
* audio and notification settings
* file paths or audio folder paths selected by the user
* cached prayer time data downloaded for app functionality
* internal logs related to app behavior, such as azan or notification trigger history

This information is stored locally and is used only to enable the app to function properly.

### 2. Personal information

myAzan does not require users to create an account and, based on its current functionality, does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or payment information.

myAzan does not sell user data to third parties.

### 3. Access to files and folders

The app may allow users to select audio files or audio folders from their computer for azan, notifications, Quran recitation, or zikr playback. This access is used only to provide the functionality requested by the user.

myAzan does not upload the user’s audio content to our servers.

### 4. Internet connectivity

myAzan may use an internet connection to download prayer time data from relevant third-party sources, such as JAKIM prayer time services, when needed for app functionality.

The app is not designed to transmit users’ personal information to us through this process.

### 5. Data storage

App data is stored locally on the user’s device. Users may remove the app and related data subject to how the operating system and installer manage application files.

### 6. Third-party services

myAzan may rely on third-party services or sources necessary for certain functions, such as prayer time data sources. The use of such third-party services is subject to their own policies and practices.

### 7. Security

We take reasonable steps to build and maintain the app with appropriate security considerations. However, no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

### 8. Children

The app is not specifically intended to collect personal information from children.

### 9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page together with the updated effective date.
